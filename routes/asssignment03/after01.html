About the Trip

A little over a year ago, I took a road trip to the western United States.

I drove over the rockies, hiked through Arches National Park, explored the Mojave Desert, walked through the redwood forests of Sequioa National Park,and camped on beaches in Norther California. These are some of the pictures from that trip.

Pictures were taken by myself with an LG G5, primarily through the wide angle secondary lense, and edited with GIMP.
